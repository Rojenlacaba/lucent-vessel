Lucent Vessel

by ATB

Overview

This brand represents our vision of ceramics and stoneware that inspire. Lucent Vessel is a name chosen with intention—to embody warmth, refinement, and timeless beauty.

Brand Vision

  • Warmth and simplicity
  • Timeless, functional beauty
  • An intentional approach to everyday living

Name Rationale

Lucent

  • Means glowing, softly radiant, emitting light
  • Conveys refinement and warmth
  • Reflects the visual and emotional qualities of our ceramics

Vessel

  • Represents containers of meaning and utility
  • Connects to our focus on ceramic objects
  • Feels timeless and poetic

Together, the name is:

  • Distinctive and memorable
  • Sophisticated yet approachable
  • Communicates our mission to curate objects that illuminate daily routines

Name Evaluation

Criterion Assessment
Relevance Strong—direct reference to ceramics
Emotional Resonance Warm, inspiring, refined
Distinctiveness Unique in the ceramics and lifestyle space
Memorability Simple and elegant
Scalability Suitable for future growth (workshops, collaborations)
Availability Instagram and domain options available

Brand Story Alignment

  • Curating pieces we personally love
  • Highlighting craftsmanship and intention
  • Eye for aesthetics

We recommend Lucent Vessel as our official brand name for its clarity, resonance, and alignment with our goals.
